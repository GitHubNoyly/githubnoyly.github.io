Web Developer

Become a web developer at Hot Beans Web and earn up to £25000 in your first year. Here at Hot Beans Web, we provide 1st rate training so that our web developers can have a successful career in website developing. As long as you have the right qualifications and the right attitude, then we will hire you!

Web developers at Hot Beans Web need a combination of graphic design skills and technical computer skills that will allow them to create particular designs on web pages. In addition to looking nice, websites need to be functional and secure. It is the responsibility of our web developers to create such sites that meet the requirements of employers or clients.

Job Details

-Wage - £25000 per year

-Work 52 hours per week

-6 weeks paid holiday

Requirements

If you want to join the Hot Beans Web family, then you will have to meet certain requirements before you can apply. If you do not meet these requirements, then feel free to check out some of our other jobs that we offer on our ‘job roles’ page. To work at Hot Beans Web, we require you to at least have:

-Bachelor's degree in Web development or related field, or relevant experience.

-Solid knowledge and experience in programming applications.

-Proficient in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and has some knowledge in PHP.

-Proficient in My SQL.

-Willing to work with collogues and clients

-High degree of independent judgment.

Work with graphics and other design tools to enhance a website

Be able to create and design page layouts (headings and paragraphs), website styling (colors and fonts), and page features (animations and pictures). Interactive features, such as submitting online payments securely.

Responsibilities

As our web developer you will have many responsibilities that you will need to make sure you can handle before applying. Many of these responsibilities will rely on basic common sense and others are skills you will be able to learn by applying for this job. These responsibilities consist of:

-Creating and testing applications for our, or our clients, websites.

-Our web developers work closely with our project managers and designers to ensure that final products adhere to predetermined budgets, scope, and designs. You will sometimes need to be able to show employers or clients a prototype of your website to help them understand what the finished product will be.

-Troubleshoot website problems.

-Maintain and update websites for clients or employers.

-Monitor website traffic and make sure that it does not exceed the bandwidth limit.

-Stay up to date on technology (such as methods on how to do certain tasks).
